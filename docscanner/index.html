App Permissions Explanation

AI Doc Scanner requests the following permissions to provide core functionality:

  • Camera: To scan documents and capture images for PDF creation

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide customer support

  • Deliver relevant advertisements (free version only)

Document and Image Privacy

Your Documents Are Private:
